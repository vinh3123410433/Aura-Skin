Our History

Aura Skin combines Vietnamese botanical traditions with modern cosmetic science to create skincare products specifically formulated for women. We're committed to natural ingredients, scientific innovation, and celebrating our cultural heritage through effective skincare solutions.

2018 2019 2020
March 2018
Company Foundation

Aura Skin was founded with a small laboratory in Hanoi, focusing on Vietnamese botanical ingredients and traditional remedies for skincare.

November 2018
Research & Development

Established R&D partnerships with local farmers and developed our first proprietary formula combining Vietnamese herbal remedies with modern skincare technology.

July 2019
First Products & Certification

Created our first product prototype with promising test results and achieved ISO 22716 certification for Good Manufacturing Practices.

January 2020
Official Product Launch

Launched our first skincare line with cleanser, toner, and moisturizer designed specifically for Vietnamese skin types.

2021 2022 2023
Q1 2021
Digital Growth & Product Expansion

Launched our e-commerce platform with personalized skin analysis tools and expanded our product line with serums, masks, and sun protection.

August 2021
Sustainability Initiative

Began our "One Product, One Tree" initiative, focusing on reforestation in Central Vietnam regions affected by deforestation.

2022
Expansion & Recognition

Opened our manufacturing facility in Ho Chi Minh City, launched our flagship store in Hanoi, and received the "Best Emerging Beauty Brand" award.

2023
Premium Line & Nationwide Reach

Introduced our premium "Aura Gold" anti-aging line and expanded to 10 retail locations with 50+ beauty retail partnerships across Vietnam.

Looking Forward: 2024 and Beyond

Aura Skin's roadmap for continued growth and innovation:

  • Regional Expansion: Southeast Asian markets by 2025
  • Research Innovation: Dedicated center for botanical research
  • Sustainability: 100% eco-friendly packaging by 2025
  • Retail Growth: 30+ locations across Vietnam by 2026
  • Environmental Goal: 100,000 trees planted by 2027
